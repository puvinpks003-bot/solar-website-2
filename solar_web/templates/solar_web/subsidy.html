Navigating regulatory landscapes is a core challenge for clean energy projects. SIP INFRA handles the entire regulatory pipeline, including TANGEDCO application management, load approvals, and Central Subsidy DBT verification.

// Standard Compliance Application Workflow

STAGE 01

Feasibility Registration

Submitting online infrastructure baseline data via national solar registry setups to receive primary load clearance validations.

STAGE 02

Technical Site Clearance

Regional electrical safety inspectors verify physical installations, testing interconnect isolation performance before connection authorization.

STAGE 03

Net Meter Integration

Replacing standard utility meters with advanced bi-directional tracking configurations to measure incoming and outgoing unit metrics.

STAGE 04

DBT Subsidy Capitalization

Submitting final verification metrics to clear straight capital fund transfers directly into registered client operating ledger frameworks.
